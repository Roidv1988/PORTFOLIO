    A little bit more of me

    Who Am I?

    Hi, I'm Roi Dvide, 35 years old from Rosh Ha'Ain. Married and father of 1. I'm a QA & Automation tester. I like to explore and test, challenge myself with new things. I have a tendency to go down to the smallest details and like to look for bugs in apps, websites and software. I am a homely, family person. I like to host and spend time with friends. I like to watch series, especially action and thrillers, and I like to cook and bake cakes. Today I work as a studio manager - graphic artist and performance artist in the field of packaging and pharma, folders, brochures, flyers, CDs, calendars, etc.

    What I Do?

    I studied design and branding at Kevim College - Kfar Saba and also courses through the IDF. Before the army, I studied film and television engineering with a specialization in video editing and after effects. In the army I was in charge of video in a military printing house. Film photography and editing, training and professional advice for new and veteran soldiers, ongoing maintenance of editing computers and editing software. After that I was charged on command and management of the department numbering between 6-10 soldiers, the guidance and training of soldiers in the field of graphic design and printing, work and service with customers, receiving projects and accompanying the soldiers to work until finished product. promoting and leading structural changes in the department, working with suppliers to purchase new machines. My work is with a variety of software such a Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere, Avid, After Effects, XD, Figma, HTML and CSS.
